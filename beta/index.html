What we do

We love getting our hands dirty.

We help universities and research institutions worldwide to develop their research prototypes, to make them practical and to build them to the highest industrial standards.

We design and develop tools for programmers. Tools that make them as productive as possible without limiting their freedom and habits.

We provide consulting in our areas - R programming language and statistics, Javascript and dynamic languages, C++, virtual machines and compilers.

Who we are

We believe in progress, that to improve something, it must first be understood, and that to understand something, a lot of experience is required. Above all, we are programmers ourselves.

We believe that great things require not only bold ideas, but solid foundations as well. We are scientists too. We learn new things on a daily basis and we help others learn what we did.

We believe that we are lucky, because we can do what we love.

Currently, we focus on R and Javascript.
